Cade Hemond

My love of animals started at a young age. My dad always kept an aquarium in our living room, and as I grew older I took an interest in having my own tanks. The fish store I began to frequent also had a selection of reptiles, ultimately leading me to crested geckos about three years ago. Since then, I have built out a collection of beautiful and fascinating creatures. I am currently a senior at Portland High School in Portland, ME, and a rising freshman at Princeton University. Unfortunately, I will not have room for my collection in my dorm room and have to find new homes for the majority of my critters. So, please take a look around the site and if any of my geckos peak your interest, please reach out!
